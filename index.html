My Projects

Tasteful

A food Web application similar to Yelp, but instead of providing food options based on location, Tasteful matches the user with restaurants based on their food preferences

SocialCeleb
SocialCeleb tracks and monitors popularity based on Instagram and Twitter followers. As more individuals become public figures and influencers, SocialCeleb is an app rising public figures and influencers use to leverage their platform to hook advertisement and marketing deals with big companies.

FireBase-Train Schedule
